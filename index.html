Our Mission

Building a Sisterhood That Transforms Africa

Sister Space Africa is more than a platform — it is a movement. Born from the understanding that Africa's progress depends on the full participation and leadership of its women, we create the spaces, connections, and opportunities that empower women and girls to lead, innovate, and thrive.

Through leadership programmes, media storytelling, economic empowerment initiatives, and transformative community events, we are cultivating a generation of women who will shape Africa's future.

Leadership Development — Mentorship, coaching, and young women empowerment programmes
Economic Empowerment — Entrepreneurship support, networking, and market access
Media & Storytelling — Podcast production, digital magazine, and leadership documentation
Community & Events — Summits, networking forums, and advocacy campaigns

Meet the Visionary

Sinako Nyombolo

Founder & CEO — Sister Space Africa

Sinako Nyombolo is a Health, Safety, and Wellness Specialist, social impact leader, and entrepreneur with a deep passion for women empowerment and community development.

As the Founder of Sister Space Africa and Idwalalethu Foundation, she leads initiatives focused on wellness, leadership, and socio-economic empowerment for women, youth, and underserved communities.

She also serves as Chairperson of the Black Management Forum (BMF) Women Empowerment Desk — Western Cape, where she drives advocacy, dialogue, and programmes advancing gender equality, GBV prevention, wellness, and women's leadership.
